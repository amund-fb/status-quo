# 3 Systems of Markets — Strategic Lens

Friendly Beast assesses every company through three interlocking systems. Use this framework to evaluate whether AI news matters for our clients and how to frame relevance notes.

## The Three Systems

### 1. Demand System — Why the market wants it
Customer need, job to be done, emotional pull, cultural relevance, brand meaning, willingness to pay, repeatability of demand. Is demand created, captured, or harvested?

### 2. Distribution System — How it reaches the market
Direct channels, wholesale/retail, e-commerce, marketplaces, media, creators, network effects, algorithmic discovery. Who controls access to the customer — the company or a gatekeeper?

### 3. Supply System — How it creates and delivers value
Product quality, operating model, cost structure, margin profile, pricing power, speed, defensibility, innovation capability, barriers to replication.

## How to apply this in the newsletter

When writing relevance notes for the **AI-Native Consultancy Plays** section, frame insights through the 3 systems:

- **Demand:** Does this AI development help our clients understand, create, or capture demand better? (e.g., AI demand forecasting, sentiment analysis, trend detection)
- **Distribution:** Does it change how products reach customers? (e.g., AI-powered marketplaces, algorithmic discovery, automated ad buying, channel optimization)
- **Supply:** Does it change how products are made, delivered, or operated? (e.g., inventory automation, cost reduction, operational efficiency, financial modeling)

The best newsletter items identify **which system is affected** and **what bottleneck it addresses**.

## Key distinctions to maintain

- Attention is not demand
- Channel access is not channel ownership
- Differentiation is not defensibility
- Product quality is not company quality
- Community energy is not commercial strength

## Strategic archetypes our clients typically are

Product machine, cultural brand, channel optimizer, premium signal brand, niche utility player, trend harvester, underpowered category leader. When writing relevance notes, think about which archetype the insight applies to.
